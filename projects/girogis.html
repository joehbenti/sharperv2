Introduction

As one of the oldest football club in Ethiopia St. George FC has been a exemplary club in for almost the rest of the teams in Ethiopia Premier League. With an old and rich history, St. George FC wanted to be the first club to offer its members a digital representation of the club. So, they set out to build the first premier league club full app with features such as membership registration and payment, club merch ecommerce, news aggregator, etc. As the UI/UX Designer lead at kifiya my responsibility was to develop a working mobile(android) prototype.
